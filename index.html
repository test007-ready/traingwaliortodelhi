4. Understanding Ticket Classes: From 1AC to Sleeper

Your choice of class transforms your journey. Here’s a quick guide:

  • 1AC (First Class AC): Private cabins, personal service, fine dining — the epitome of luxury. Approx. ₹1,330–₹1,595.
  • 2AC: Spacious berths with privacy curtains, reading lights. ₹805–₹1,310.
  • 3AC: Air‑conditioned comfort at a sensible price. ₹575–₹950.
  • Executive Chair Car (EC): Daytime opulence on Shatabdi/Vande Bharat. ₹965–₹1,440.
  • Chair Car (CC): Comfortable seating with meal service. ₹495–₹965.

12. Seasonal Magic: When to Travel

October to March offers crisp, pleasant weather, making the train Gwalior to Delhi ride incredibly comfortable. The winter fog can delay trains, but the misty landscapes are hauntingly beautiful. Monsoon (July‑September) brings dramatic skies and verdant fields. Avoid peak summer afternoons unless you’re in AC class.
